''79% of Employers want students to do more projects''

GigRoot is an online marketplace for college student services. Its mission is to provide students with a platform to grow their skills, get exposure to employers, and deliver needed services around campus.

Students, especially design and engineering students, are facing a difficult problem of creating a portfolio to demonstrate their skills. Meanwhile, employers are increasingly looking for more involved students with applicable skills for the workplace. According to a recent study by the Association of American Colleges and Universities, 79% of employers believe that college students need to put the MOST emphasis on the application of knowledge and skills in real-world settings. No longer can students rely purely on good grades.

As a fellow college student, I understand the challenge of getting noticed by employers and the struggle of finding help on projects and courses. GigRoot will be the solution to this problem by connecting students with each other to do great things. If you want to tell me what you're working on or give some input; join the discussion >
